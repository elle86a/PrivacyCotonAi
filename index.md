# Privacy Policy

This browser extension does not collect, store, or sell personally identifiable user data.

The extension accesses and processes visible content on web pages (such as social media comments) only when explicitly triggered by the user, for the sole purpose of generating a contextual reply.

Any text processed may be temporarily transmitted to a backend service for response generation and is not stored, profiled, or shared with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.

No authentication data, financial data, personal identifiers, or browsing history are collected.
